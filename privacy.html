Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

WireRules is a network firewall and traffic inspection tool that runs entirely on your device. We are committed to your privacy — the app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

WireRules does not collect any data from your device. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry is sent
  • No network traffic data leaves your device
  • No crash reports are transmitted automatically
  • No advertising identifiers are used

Data Storage

All data generated by WireRules — including traffic logs, filter rules, and application settings — is stored locally on your device and is never transferred to external servers.

Network Access

WireRules requires network access to function as a firewall and traffic inspector. All packet inspection occurs locally on your device. No intercepted traffic is forwarded to, stored on, or accessible by any third-party server.

Third-Party Services

WireRules does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at devwirerulesorg.
