Features

Everything you need to secure your network

Powerful deep packet inspection with an intuitive interface. See what's happening and take control.

Deep Packet Inspection

Inspect TLS, QUIC, HTTP, and DNS traffic in real-time. Extract hostnames from encrypted connections, including IETF QUIC and Google gQUIC.

Smart Blocking

Block domains, IPs, and ports with flexible rules. Wildcard patterns, CIDR ranges, and per-app filtering. Hot-reload rules without restarting.

Cross-Platform

One engine across Android, iOS, macOS, Windows, and Linux. Same DPI logic, same accuracy, everywhere you need it.

Real-Time Monitoring

See every connection as it happens. Track bytes, packets, hostnames, and protocols with live flow events streamed to the UI.

TLS Fingerprinting

JA4 fingerprints identify client applications and detect anomalies. Know exactly what's connecting from your network.

IPv4 & IPv6

Full dual-stack support with IPv6 extension header traversal. Works on modern networks out of the box, no configuration needed.
